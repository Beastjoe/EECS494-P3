4 Players are divided into red team and blue team and their goal is to collect coins around the map and place them into their base.
Players can throw their team member as ball to hit the other team, and when one player gets hit, he will be stunned and lose coins.

Control:
Left trigger - Hold to place coin (must stand in the base of the team)
Left shoulder - Get prepared to be thrown
Right shoulder - Hold the preparing team member on the top of head
Right trigger - Throw
Left joystick - Move
Right joystick - Change direction
Enter(keyboard) - Restart
